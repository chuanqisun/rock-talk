# Venue design spec

## Entrance area

- A3 posters on the wall, including artist statement and instruction of interaction

## Listening area

- 3 wall mounted LCD screen, showing rocks and their back story
- Next to screen, a headphone hanging on the wall, allow user to listen to the rock sharing its memories of interacting with the user

## Interaction area

- 1 plinthed rock sculpture
- 1 microphone and 1 headset
- A screen to let user select from a predefined list of topics
- 3 minute session per visitor
- A hidden computer inside the plinth to run the rock application

## Exit area

- Provide business cards and contact information for further engagement
